My Speech

A room of one's own

I was assigned to do a speech about discrimination. I created a speech inspired by the essay of Virgina Woolf, A room of one's own. It's about how women are limited due to mysoginistic rules created by society. It's a very soul-touching topic for me. I will always be open to talk about feminism and the sufferings of my fellow women.
